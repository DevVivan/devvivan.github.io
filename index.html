About Me


Hey there! My name is Vivan. I'm a high-school student, web developer, avid reader, keyboardist, roboticist, electronics enthusiast and much more.

I have always been fascinated by the world of technology. Whether it's been printing my first “Hello World” program, or tinkering around with the Terminal, the thrill behind building something from scratch has continued to drive my passion for technology.

Today, I am quite proficient in frontend web development, where I bring my ideas to life through my code. I also enjoy producing hobby robots in my free time and creating projects that can help better the community around me.
